WHAT IS RBCL?

Rising bharat cricket league, managed by shivay sports management pvt ltd, shivay sports management pvt ltd is registered. Ministry of Company Affairs, rising Bharat cricket league's dream is to grow sports, its vision is to support and promote, convert unprofessional to professional players in India.

RBCL mission and vision . RBCL The talent hunt, focused on aged between 11and 25, aims to identify and nurture promising talent across the country. With the involvement of qualified coaches, who bring a wealth of expertise and experience, the selected participants will have the chance to learn from the best. By supervising the event, these coaches will not only assess the skills and potential of the young PLEYERS but also provide guidance and mentorship to aid their development.

The core vision of RBCL is to use cricket sports as a universal tool for empowering the underprivileged children and youth of the society. Mission: build nation through sports RBCL promotes sports culture among the vulnerable society to change their lives to come out of poverty to the main stream.
