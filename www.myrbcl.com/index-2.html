RISING BHARAT CRICEKT LEAGUE

Welcome to the Rising Bharat Cricket League, managed by Shivay Sports Management Pvt Ltd, a registered entity with the Ministry of Company Affairs. Our league is not just a sporting event; it's a celebration of the game's spirit, uniting communities through thrilling cricket competitions. Committed to fair play and sportsmanship, we provide a platform for both aspiring and seasoned cricketers.

 

Shivay Sports Management Pvt Ltd is dedicated to fostering the growth of sports, with a particular vision to support and promote amateur players in India. The Rising Bharat Cricket League dreams of contributing to this vision.

 

Our Cricket League serves as a platform that celebrates the spirit of the game, creating an inclusive and competitive environment that fosters camaraderie among teams and fans.

 

Our mission is to promote the love of cricket, nurture talent, and showcase the excitement of the sport. Join us in this cricketing journey, where each match tells a unique story, and every player plays a crucial role in building the league's legacy. Let's come together to play, compete, and celebrate the extraordinary world of cricket.

League superheroes SELECTION PROCESS

LEVEL 1 TRIAL

League superheroes-Rising Bharat Cricket League has come up with a platform where players can perform, play for their state, get sponsorship and have a good experience. If your age is above 11 years then you can register. Registration fee starts from Rs 599. Or website www.myLeague superheroes.com Total 6 further groups are Under 13, Under 16, Under 19 Under 23, Under 25 and Above 25.

LEVEL 2 SELECTION LEAGUE MATCH

After passing the trial, you will be given a league match to play. In the league match, teams of the players will be formed according to their age groups. All the teams will be given four matches to play. The match fee will include three T20 and one T10 match in the four matches. Your name will be added to the team only after paying the fee.

LEVEL 3 STATE LEAGUE MATCH

The player who achieves his selection criteria in the league match will qualify for the State League. ODI matches will be given to all the teams in the State League. There will be Day and Night matches. Food refreshments will be provided to you during the match. Many facilities will be provided in the State League. You can be charged for this if you perform well in the state league and fulfill the given criteria. If you perform well in four league matches and 5 state matches, then you qualify for the National League. It's absolutely free for you to play national league.

LEVEL 4 NATIONAL LEAGUE AND SPONSORSHIP

National league benefits National league will have 12 to 15 teams, each team will have 15 players, all matches will come live on YouTube, the final will come on your TV, cash price will be given to the winning team, sponsorship will be given to the top 15 players for 2.6 years. The value of sponsorship will be up to 15 lakhs. Food, stay and many more benefits will be given.
